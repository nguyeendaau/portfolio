Consumer Research & Growth Strategy for Gen Z Expansion

Led a 5-member team to design and execute a marketing intelligence study for Ddreamer — a Vietnamese jewellery brand pausing marketing since 2019. The goal: re-enter the market targeting Gen Z (18–24) while retaining Millennial loyalty, with a 10% sales growth target and 3:1 ROI in 3 months.

828 respondents HCMC + Hanoi Dual-persona framework 4P recommendations Team of 5

Key Insights

Age & willingness to pay were the two strongest predictors of purchase behaviour — used to build dual personas: "The Current" (Millennials) vs "The Potential" (Gen Z).

BNPL is a conversion unlock for Gen Z — financial flexibility directly increases purchase likelihood in the 18–24 segment.

Micro-KOC over macro-influencer — authenticity outperforms reach; 55% of Gen Z trust peer recommendations over celebrity endorsement.

Ddreamer's untapped moat: the only workshop preserving traditional Vietnamese silversmithing — a powerful brand story not yet leveraged in marketing.
